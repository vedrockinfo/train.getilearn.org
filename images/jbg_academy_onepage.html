Why ALfA × Hi PATH?

A paradigm shift — not just another method. Below are the contrasts that matter.

Purpose of Education

Then: Sorting for exams & jobs. Now: Life, soul, wellbeing and world citizenship plus strong literacy & numeracy.

Role of Teacher

Then: Instructor & controller. Now: Galvaniser who designs processes and lets learners lead.

Role of Learner

Then: Passive listener. Now: Active co-creator — pairs, peer-teaching and parent engagement.

Materials & Curriculum

Then: Fixed textbooks. Now: Stretchable kits, buddy books and routines children extend.

Assessment & Feedback

Then: Term exams & ranks. Now: Ongoing reflect–perfect cycles, portfolios and growth evidence.

Motivation & Climate

Then: Rewards & fear. Now: Intrinsic motivation and emotional safety for every child.

Programmes

Training that is lived, practised, refined and documented — not a slide deck you forget.

Workshops vs Process Learning

Then: one-off theory. Now: short modules with classroom practice and reflection loops.

Hearing vs Living

Others talk about active learning. We make you experience ALfA as a learner.

Generic Tips vs Parakh-linked Design

We compare real textbooks with buddy books to redesign pages, assessments and prompts.

Homework vs Home Stretch

We design child-led home engagements where parents record or observe.

Worksheets vs Creative Prompts

We use prompts (and AI as assistant) to create thinking-rich tasks.

Marks vs Portfolios

Your outputs: redesigned materials, videos, reflection notes and classroom evidence.

Professional Pathways

Which professional do you want to become? Each card shows the before → after.

Transformative Teacher

Before: delivers textbook. After: designs learner-led, pair-based classes and includes every child.

Trainer / Galvaniser

Before: lectures teachers. After: models ALfA with adults and co-designs practice.

Content Designer

Before: teacher-centred sheets. After: child-centred prompts, STREAM links and buddy processes.

Researcher

Before: distant research. After: embedded, classroom-led studies feeding back into practice.

Principal / Leader

Before: timetables & discipline. After: pedagogical leader aligning the school to ALfA.

Supervisor / QA

Before: checklist policing. After: supportive observation for teacher growth.
